GSMI Fellows Program:

The GBBC partnered with seven academic institutions actively involved in blockchain and digital asset research to launch the GSMI Fellows Program, an eight-month fellowship for exceptional students from partners institutions to contribute to GSMI 2.0 research and analysis.

Partner Institutions

  • KAIST (Daejon, South Korea)
  • Miami University Farmer School of Business (Oxford, Ohio)
  • Portland State University (Portland, Oregon)
  • Singapore Management University School of Law (Singapore)
  • University of Bahrain (Zallaq, Bahrain)
  • University of Dundee (Dundee, Scotland)
  • University of Wyoming Center for Blockchain and Digital Innovation (Laramie, Wyoming)
